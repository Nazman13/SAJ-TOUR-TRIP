Terms and policies

Short, clear, and presentation-friendly. You can adjust based on lecturer instructions.

Booking and payment policy Important
A booking is confirmed after agreement on date, group size, and itinerary scope. Payment terms follow the final quotation and can include down payment for securing accommodation and boats.
Reschedule policy Flexible
Reschedule is possible depending on availability. Sea and weather conditions can affect the itinerary. SAJ TRIP prioritizes safety and will propose the closest equivalent schedule when needed.
Participant responsibility Safety
Participants must follow guide instructions during snorkeling and boat sessions. Bring personal medicine if needed. Keep respectful behavior during village visits.

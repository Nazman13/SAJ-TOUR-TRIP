Travel period and booking window

Ideal travel period

  • Good overall conditions: October to April.
  • Generally calmer waves: November to February.
  • Program always depends on local weather and sea conditions.

Recommended booking period

  • Book 30–60 days before departure for better flight options.
  • Earlier booking helps secure boat and homestay capacity for your group.
  • Campus groups can use lead time for savings, fundraising, or proposal submission.

Sample itinerary 5D4N

Day 1: Arrival and welcome session

Arrival at Sorong, boat transfer to Raja Ampat, check-in, briefing, free time, and rest.

Day 2: Piaynemo viewpoint and lagoon

Boat trip, viewpoint hike, panorama session, lagoon exploration, snorkeling, then return before sunset.

Day 3: Snorkeling and sand bank

Full day snorkeling at selected spots and sand bank session for group documentation.

Day 4: Village visit and flexible time

Light cultural visit, then flexible blocks for rest, walking, or photography.

Day 5: Check-out and return

Boat transfer to Sorong, flight home, closing session and group photo.

Itinerary may adjust due to weather, safety, and flight schedules while keeping key highlights.

Price and inclusions

Student price status

The package uses a price on request system. This allows your campus group to receive a quote that matches:

  • Travel month
  • Number of participants
  • Departure city

Inclusions

  • 4 nights accommodation in shared rooms
  • Meals based on final itinerary
  • Boat and local transport for island hopping and village visit
  • Destination entrance and conservation fees (scheduled)
  • Local guide and UNITour trip leader
  • Basic group documentation support at key spots

Exclusions

  • Return flight tickets to Sorong
  • Personal expenses and souvenirs
  • Optional extensions outside the main program
  • Travel insurance (optional add-on)

Terms and policies

Important reminders

  • Schedule may change due to weather and sea conditions.
  • Participants must follow safety instructions for water activities.
  • Life jackets are strongly recommended during boat rides and snorkeling.
  • Campus groups should assign a coordinator for smooth communication.

General terms

  • Final availability depends on accommodation, boats, and flights at booking time.
  • Deposit is required to secure slot, remaining payment follows an agreed schedule.
  • Participant data may be needed for ticketing and local registration.

Refund and reschedule

  • Refund and reschedule follow airline and local operator policies.
  • Deposit is typically non-refundable but may be transferable within the same group.

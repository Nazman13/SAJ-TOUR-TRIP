Sample Itinerary 5D4N

Itinerary can be adjusted according to your flight time and group preferences, but this is the general flow for the UNITour Raja Ampat open trip.

Day 1
Arrival and first island welcome

Arrival at the meeting point, transfer to homestay, welcome briefing, and light exploration around the area. Evening chill by the pier and group introduction session.

Day 2
Piaynemo viewpoint and lagoon

Boat trip to Piaynemo, short hike to the famous viewpoint, photo session, lagoon exploration, and snorkeling in nearby clear water spots. Evening rest at homestay.

Day 3
Snorkeling and sand bank day

Full day for snorkeling at selected coral reef spots, sand bank time for group photos, and relaxed beach activities. Simple sunset gathering to close the day.

Day 4
Village visit and free exploration

Visit a local village to learn about daily life, culture, and stories from the community. Free time in the afternoon for short walks, more photos, or rest.

Day 5
Goodbye Raja Ampat

Check out, boat transfer back to the meeting point, and flight preparation. Group photo, quick recap, and closing notes from UNITour trip leader.

Note
Flexibility and weather

The order of activities may change due to weather conditions or safety considerations. Safety will always be the top priority for every decision on the field.

Frequently asked questions

Here are some common questions from students before they join the trip.

Can I join alone or do I need a group?
You can join alone, with a friend, or as a full campus group. For the best student price, it is recommended to come in a small group so we can optimize the boat and room sharing.
Do I need to be a strong swimmer?
Basic swimming skill is helpful, but we also choose spots that are friendly for beginners. Life jackets are prepared and the guide will brief you before each water activity.
How is the payment system for students?
Usually there is a deposit to secure your slot, then the remaining payment can be completed in stages before departure. Full details will be shared through WhatsApp when you send your plan.

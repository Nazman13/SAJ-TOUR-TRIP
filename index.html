Package overview

UNITour Raja Ampat 5D4N Student Open Trip is designed for university life. The package uses shared rooms, group boat arrangements, and a compact program so students can experience the best parts of Raja Ampat without starting from zero.

Package name UNITour Raja Ampat 5D4N Student Open Trip
Trip type Open trip for small to medium student groups
Recommended group size 8 – 16 participants per group
Package highlights
  • Piaynemo viewpoint with blue lagoon panorama.
  • Island hopping with curated snorkeling spots.
  • Sandbank photoshoot session for group content.
  • Local village visit to see daily life and culture.
Sample itinerary 5D4N
Day 1 – Arrival

Arrive in Sorong, boat transfer, check in homestay, welcome session, and rest.

Day 2 – Piaynemo

Viewpoint hike, lagoon exploration, and island hopping.

Day 3 – Snorkeling

Snorkeling at selected coral reef spots and sandbank session.

Day 4 – Village visit

Visit local village, flexible time for content and rest.

Day 5 – Return

Check out, boat transfer back to Sorong, flight to home city.
